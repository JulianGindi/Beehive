What Do You Love?

Have you ever watched a YouTube video 10 times? Do you find yourself constantly referencing that one article on the web? BeeHive allows you to store and share these things with your friends. Check out what we have going on here - We think you will love it.

Philosophy

Inspired by Robin Sloan's iPhone essay on distinguishing things on the Internet that you love, BeeHive allows you to store those things and share them with your friends.
